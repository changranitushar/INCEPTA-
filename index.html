Who We Are

Reengineering the
entrepreneurial future

INCEPTA — The Startup & Innovation Cell is the officially recognised entrepreneurship body of the Faculty of Commerce, Banaras Hindu University. We are novel thinkers, builders and leaders committed to creating an entrepreneurial, equitable and innovative future.

We don't just teach entrepreneurship — we simulate it. Through high-pressure ideathons, real mentorship from founders and VCs, and deep immersion into cutting-edge sectors, we equip students to thrive in an AI-augmented economy.

Our ecosystem is built for the native generation — those who will define India's next wave of innovation.

Our Mission

Relevance for the
Native Generation

  • 01 AI-Augmented Readiness — Equipping students for an economy where adaptability & strategic thinking outpace rote expertise.
  • 02 ESG-Aligned Entrepreneurship — Championing climate-conscious ventures aligned with India's net-zero commitments.
  • 03 Startup Ecosystem Fluency — Building literacy in Startup India, DPIIT, funding mechanisms & regulatory landscape.

  • 05 Data Literacy — Embedding analytics as core competencies for evidence-driven business decisions.
